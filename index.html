About Me

Engineering Leader
turned Strategic PM

Bridging deep technical expertise with business strategy to deliver mission-critical solutions.

I'm a PMP® Certified Project & Product Manager with over 10 years of hands-on experience in embedded systems, IoT, and automotive electronics. I currently lead an 8-member cross-functional R&D team at Klug Avalon Mechatronics Pvt. Ltd., delivering Safety-Critical Safe Load Indicator (SLI) systems for cranes.

My background spans the full product lifecycle — from firmware architecture and hardware design to stakeholder management, P&L ownership, and regulatory compliance including DO-178B and MISRA-C standards — a rare combination in the Indian PM landscape.

"I translate complex engineering challenges into structured business outcomes — on time, within budget, and compliant with the highest safety standards."

Beyond the corporate world, I actively mentor engineering students, have conducted workshops at MSRIT, BMSIT, and PSR Engineering College, and volunteer with the PMI Pune Chapter.
