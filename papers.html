About My Research

My research work was conducted primarily during my tenure as a Research Associate at PESIT under the prestigious Naval Research Board (NRB) project — focused on underwater acoustic communication systems using ultrasonic signals and MATLAB-based signal processing.

This work directly contributed to improving data accuracy by 95% through algorithm development and experimental validation. The research spanned robotics, signal processing, IoT communication, and embedded security systems — resulting in 7 published papers across national conferences and international journals.

I also mentored undergraduate research teams during this period, a practice I've continued throughout my career through workshops at MSRIT, BMSIT, RRIT, and PSR Engineering College.
