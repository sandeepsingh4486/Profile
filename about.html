Who I Am

Engineering Leader
turned Strategic PM

I'm a PMP® Certified Project Manager and XLRI-Certified Product Manager with 10+ years of cross-functional experience spanning Embedded Systems, IoT, Semiconductor, and Automation domains. I currently lead an 8-member cross-functional engineering team at Klug Avalon Mechatronics Pvt. Ltd., delivering Safety-Critical Safe Load Indicator (SLI) systems for cranes — achieving 40% crane downtime reduction and 100% on-time delivery across 5+ major projects.

My background spans the full product lifecycle — from firmware architecture and PCB hardware design to stakeholder management, P&L ownership, and regulatory compliance including DO-178B and MISRA-C standards. This rare combination of hands-on technical depth and structured PM discipline makes me a strong fit for both Senior PM and Associate Product Manager roles.

"I translate complex engineering challenges into structured business outcomes — on time, within budget, and compliant with the highest safety standards."

Beyond the corporate world, I actively mentor engineering students, have conducted IoT and MATLAB workshops at MSRIT, BMSIT, RRIT, and PSR Engineering College, and volunteer with the PMI Pune Chapter. I'm also a Udemy instructor for MATLAB basics and hold an NCC 'B' Certificate. In my personal time, I enjoy rock climbing, rappelling, and trekking.
